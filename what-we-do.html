What we do

We're focused on resources the world needs to develop responsibly and decarbonise

Products

From iron ore and metallurgical coal that create steel for construction, to copper enabling the next generation of renewable infrastructure, our products are the building blocks of the modern world.

Global locations

At more than 90 locations around the world our teams work on extracting, processing and transporting commodities needed to meet demand from the global megatrends of decarbonisation, electrification, urbanisation and a rising population.
